核心文法：主格、受格、所有格

1. 主格：關係代名詞後面直接接動詞

The girl who lives next door is kind.

who 後面接 lives，代表 who 是 lives 的主詞。

2. 受格：關係子句裡主詞已經出現

The song which I like is old.

I 是主詞，like 後面缺受詞，所以 which 是受詞。

3. 所有格：whose + 名詞

The man whose car is red is my teacher.

whose car = the man's car。

where / when 的判斷技巧

用 where：子句缺「在那裡」

This is the school where I studied.

I studied 不缺受詞，只缺地點。

不用 where：子句缺受詞

This is the school which I visited.

visited 後面缺受詞，所以用 which / that。

用 when：子句缺「在那時」

I remember the year when I moved here.

I moved here 不缺受詞，只缺時間背景。
